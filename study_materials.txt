Legal & Judicial Terminology Questions:

What is the meaning of stare decisis? - A legal principle that obligates courts to follow historical cases when making a ruling on a similar case.

What does amicus curiae mean? - Friend of the court; someone who is not a party to a case who offers information to assist the court in deciding a matter before it.

Professional Standards & Ethics Questions:

How long must a court reporter maintain backup audio files? - 3 years from the date of the proceeding.

Can a court reporter serve as a notary public in the same proceeding? - No, it creates a conflict of interest.

Transcription Standards Questions:

What is the minimum speed requirement for real-time court reporting? - 225 WPM.

How should speaker identification be formatted in Q&A? - The speaker should be identified in all caps followed by a colon.
